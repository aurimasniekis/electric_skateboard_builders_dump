The cells start to fire from inside again and again. 
I was waiting for 1-2min than used the salt water, which finally stopped everything from burning.
I wouldnâ€™t use the water in first place as it would just run away fast. If the cells already close to burn out it helps very good.
Canâ€™t say if you really need salt water or if in my case just water would be enough.
All in all it took me five to seven minutes till all fire was shut down.
