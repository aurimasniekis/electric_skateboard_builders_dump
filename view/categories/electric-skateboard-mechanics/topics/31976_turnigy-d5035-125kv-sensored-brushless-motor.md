## \#39 Posted by: Nordle Posted at: 2018-01-29T18:24:32.132Z Reads: 154

```
Hall sensors may be dead. If you want to know try to power your Hall sensor wires with 3.3V on VCC and GND and measure the voltage on the 3 sense wires while spinning the can manually, if the voltage goes up and down on each one they're fine, if one of them is stuck in high or low that one is toast.
```
