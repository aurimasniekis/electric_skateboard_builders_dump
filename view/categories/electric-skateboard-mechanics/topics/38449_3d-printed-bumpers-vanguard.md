# 3D-printed bumpers (Vanguard)

### Replies: 1 Views: 415

## \#1 Posted by: pakue Posted at: 2017-11-15T22:18:56.819Z Reads: 80

```
As I found myself scuffing up the nose and tail of my longboard I decided to make some bumpers for it. The plastic used is a TPE blend so they have the consistency of rubber. The way I mounted them they also serve as 5mm risers.
The 3D files can be found on [thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2647037).
```
