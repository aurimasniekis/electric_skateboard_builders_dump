```
Wow, I was in the EXACT same position as you a week ago. I got 3 screws off with no heat, then stripped the fourth. 

What I did was first get a small pick and clean out the screw head out of any debris from the stripping. Once the screw was clean, I applied almost a minute of low heat from a heat gun.
```
