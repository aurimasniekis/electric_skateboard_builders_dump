Hello Everyone!
I just finished my first build and it works great! I learned everything from reading on this website and thought it was my duty to share what I've done. 
I used: 
2 X Zippy 3S 5000mah 
Enertion Moror Mount 
SK3 Turnigy Aerodrive 5065-236kv
Mamba Monster 2 ESC 
Home made fiberglass enclosure
83mm Flywheels 
40T wheel pulley
16T motor pulley
Bamboo Longboard 
Flysky 2.4 GHz Transmitter 
Enertion Trucks 

Top speed aprox 20mph
range:6-8miles
