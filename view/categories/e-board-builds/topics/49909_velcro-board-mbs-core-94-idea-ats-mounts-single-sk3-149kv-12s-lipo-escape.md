```
I finally decided on my components. Thanks @Der6FingerJo, @rich, @AndyBigD and everyone else for help! Starting my "Velcro Board" build page. First, why Velcro Board? I want to build an electric eMTB that can be easily converted to normal MTB, so that I can use the motor for getting to kiting spots, remove the motor/battery/ESC and use the MTB for kiting. More about that [here](http://www.electric-skateboard.builders/t/mtb-with-easy-to-add-remove-motor/49317). It will be something that's easy to put together and take apart again, same as velcro. Plus, I think the build will rely on actual velcro heavily. :slight_smile:   And I kinda like the name. So Velcro Board it is... or it will be... I hope.

To do the conversion, the plan is to have two hangers and two pairs of wheels -- motorized and normal -- and swap them.

I will keep this page updated as I progress in my build.

For now, here's the list of components and expected budget:
- MBS Core 94 (already have it; used one for ~280 EUR)
- Extra set of MBS ATS 12 trucks with MBS wheels (80 EUR)
- [Idea MBS ATS 12 mount](https://www.electric-skateboard.builders/t/motormounts-kits-for-trampa-e-mtb/15502) for 1 motor (176 EUR)
- [Turnigy Aerodrive SK3 - 6374-149KV Brushless Outrunner Motor](https://hobbyking.com/en_us/turnigy-aerodrive-sk3-6374-149kv-brushless-outrunner-motor.html) (62 EUR)
- 4x [ZIPPY Flightmax 8000mAh 3S1P 30C Lipo Pack with XT90](https://hobbyking.com/en_us/zippy-flightmax-8000mah-3s1p-30c-lipo-pack-xt90.html) (125 EUR)
- [ESCape](http://www.electric-skateboard.builders/t/hw6-4-based-esc-escape/37579) (~172 EUR)
- [HobbyKing HK-GT2B 3CH 2.4GHz Transmitter and Receiver](https://hobbyking.com/en_us/hobbykingr-tmhk-gt2b-3ch-2-4ghz-transmitter-and-receiver-w-rechargable-li-ion-battery-1.html) (20 EUR)
- 2x [IMAX B6 DC Charger 5A 50W (Copy)](https://hobbyking.com/en_us/imax-b6-dc-charger-5a-50w-copy.html) (31 EUR)
- Power supply for the chargers (~15-20 EUR)
- Various small parts, wires, connectors, etc (~25 EUR)
- Shipping (~40 EUR)

Total: ~1000 EUR
```
