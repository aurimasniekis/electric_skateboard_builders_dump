I have finally decided to post all that I have ordered for my first build in hopes to help others build there own e-board. To start off, purchased a drop through deck, added galaxy grip tape because why not make it pretty. With shipping it was $63.28

https://www.skateshred.com/wholesale-blank-longboard-decks/blank-drop-down-longboard-decks/40-75-x-9-75-drop-down-blank-deck.html

My “brains of the board” Vesc Dual 6.6 based off of v6, total cost with shipping and drum roll please...... discount code welcomeback, was 259.55
https://flipsky.net/collections/electronic-products/products/dual-fsesc6-6-based-upon-vesc6-with-aluminum-heatsink

Battery and motors I bought from @KaramQ
Total cost for me was $340 with shipping 
Motors were turnigy Sk3 6374
Battery is 10s12p, built from these cells, https://voltaplex.com/lg-mf1-18650-battery-lgdamf11865

Trucks wheels and mounts were all bought here in this package from @dickyho cost me $170
https://rover.ebay.com/rover/0/0/0?mpre=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.ebay.com%2Fulk%2Fitm%2F303057842183

Last but not least for sure, at least I hope it’s not the least, my remote. Vanpro remote bought off amazon, $27.80 
vanpro Electric Skateboard DIY V2 Edition 2.4G Mini Wireless Remote Control Receiver Transmit 80 Meters Away Wireless https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07L498PZR/ref=cm_sw_r_cp_api_i_C11uDb565T342

The battery has its own enclosure, 
https://www.wayfair.com/storage-organization/pdp/b-w-type-2000-outdoor-case-with-si-foam-bwh1129.html

The VESC I plan on printing an enclosure for it out of orange PETG, will update as soon as all the parts get here!!
