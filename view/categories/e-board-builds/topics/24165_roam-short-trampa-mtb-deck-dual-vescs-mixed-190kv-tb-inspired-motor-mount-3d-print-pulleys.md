Here is my electric mountainboard build that was started mid-April 2017. Have gotten 3 days of riding in and it's so much fun! Going to the MTB park next weekend!

_**DETAILS ABOUT THE BUILD:**_

* Deck: [Trampa Short MTB](http://www.trampaboards.com/35-short-trampa-deck-on-infinity-trucks-hypa-wheels-velcro-bindings--666-mountainboard-p-12331.html) (Cheapest one :grin: )
* Battery (3): [LiPo 3S 5000mAh 30C Zippy](https://hobbyking.com/en_us/zippy-flightmax-5000mah-3s1p-30c.html) 
* ESC Primary: [OllinBoardCo. VESC 4.12](http://www.ollinboardcompany.com/product/vedder-s-speed-controller)
* ESC Secondary: [Torqueboards VESC 4.12](diy-electric-skateboard-kits-parts/vesc-the-best-electric-skateboard-esc/)
* Motor (2): [Torqueboards 6355 190KV](diy-electric-skateboard-kits-parts/electric-skateboard-motor-6355-190kv/)
* Motor Mount: Custom mount based off Torqueboard's V4 mount
* Total Cost: **$952**

_**CURRENTLY WORKING ON:**_

* Working on a carbon fiber VESC enclosure
* Motor mount guards
* Designing a logo

**_ONLINE CALCULATORS I USED:_**

[Speed Calculator](http://toddy616.blogspot.com/2013/07/electric-skateboard-calculator.html)
[Center Distance Calculator](https://sdp-si.com/eStore/CenterDistanceDesigner)
[Used the tables from BeltingOnline to design the 72T pulley](http://www.beltingonline.com/)
