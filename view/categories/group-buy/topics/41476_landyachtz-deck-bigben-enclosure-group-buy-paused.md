## \#61 Posted by: deucesdown Posted at: 2018-02-19T15:28:06.650Z Reads: 221

```
Lifepo4 is 3.6v full, 3.3v nominal. A123 is a premium lifepo4 that can do unusually high rates of charge and discharge, and can take a lot of different types of abuse. Lowest I've seen is about $6.5/cell.
```
