## \#59 Posted by: rey8801 Posted at: 2019-03-12T08:28:18.039Z Reads: 178

```
I really like them despite the DD solution.

I asked the company whether they changed the PU sleeve based of our comments during the last GB. Basically only in one case, I think also due to a wrong winding for which they send a new stator in replacement, the metal screws digged inside the PU sleeve once they were really hot.
```
