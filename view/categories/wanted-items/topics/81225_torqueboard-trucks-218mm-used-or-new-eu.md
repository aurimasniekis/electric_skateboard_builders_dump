I'm not sure where you are getting your information from but you can't avoid paying VAT and customs duties by importing through Ireland and It is standard practice to pay VAT on goods and services if you are a VAT registered company and you are required by law to charge VAT to customers.

When paying an invoice from a UK based company or when importing a large order of stuff from outside of the EU the shipping company will demand the charges upfront or very soon after delivery, companies can reclaim the VAT amount later on but they often need to have paid the bill months beforehand plus there is a large overhead in terms of time spent and accountancy costs associated with being VAT registered which further adds to overheads.

Nothing is on perpetual sale, although I have been slack about updating the prices of late, this has now been fixed.
