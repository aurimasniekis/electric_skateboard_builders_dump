## \#5 Posted by: b264 Posted at: 2018-07-20T07:42:40.443Z Reads: 307

```
They are sort-of a hybrid.  They are not press-fit (I wish they were) but as the inside of the pulley fits bearings, you can lightly tighten the bolts.  I suppose you could even JB Weld the bolts in and snip the top of them off so there is no ring on the outside of the wheel if you used the bearings inside the pulleys

In short, there is no "good" 40T or 44T wheel pulley with inserts.  When there is, I hope it will be a kegel-style core for the new @torqueboards wheels and the Boa Constrictors
```
