## \#2 Posted by: Moros Posted at: 2018-07-07T15:20:38.752Z Reads: 175

```
The [used items for sale](https://www.electric-skateboard.builders/c/electric-skateboard-parts-market/used-items-for-sale) section is [open to all members](https://www.electric-skateboard.builders/t/about-the-used-items-for-sale-category/3587/3).

The [new items category](https://www.electric-skateboard.builders/c/electric-skateboard-parts-market/new-items-for-sale) is open to [members Level 2 and above](https://www.electric-skateboard.builders/t/about-the-new-items-for-sale-category/3588)

The [group buy](https://www.electric-skateboard.builders/c/electric-skateboard-parts-market/group-buy) section is only for [members Level 3 and above](https://www.electric-skateboard.builders/t/about-the-group-buys-category/3586).

For more information on discourse levels and the requirements for each, you can [read this post](https://blog.discourse.org/2018/06/understanding-discourse-trust-levels/) made on the the discourse blog.
```
