```
----------


Time to start the build thread.....
-------------------------------
*Some brief history first though!*

I haven't done a build thread for a while, actually I think the last one I did was the [Hobo Board][2]

**The HOBO BOARD Specs**
1. Dual 6355 190KV R-SPEC MOTORS
2. Early SPACE Cell Prototype
3. Custom Made 50A Current limited Flier 10S ESC - (Vesc wasn't available)
4. Earthwing Superglider Deck - with high-tech shrink wrap!

What I didn't make clear in the HOBO build thread was it was basically an early RAPTOR prototype. The HOBO build was made primarily for testing the early SPACE Cell prototype.

Afterwards, I modified the HOBO board with DUAL VESC from the first ever VESC batch made. I then made this video demonstrating the performance of that build.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=59d9JLXF2TI

**What I didn't have then, that I do have now is**
1. Carbon Fiber Deck
2. Compact VESC Remote Controller.
3. Lazer Cut Grip

It's been well over 12 months of developing, designing, testing and organizing factories to produce  the various RAPTOR parts! Now finally all the pieces of the puzzle are coming together.
------------------------------------------------------------------------

*NOTE: Technically I'm still waiting on some parts so that I can complete all of the pre-ordered Raptors! YES feel free to laugh & joke... but that's the reality of running a small business doing micro scale custom manufacturing in a rapidly growing newly developing industry..* 

One word describes it "DELAYS"

**The parts I am waiting for.**
1. Lazer cut grip - in transit
2. Wireless Hand Controller - final assembly stage
3. VESC - final assembly stage
4. Extra Stock of CF Decks - Just in case i sell lots more RAPTORS

The parts listed above are about 7 days away! Considering the complexity of the product I am building I think one week of delay is acceptable.
```

## \#64 Posted by: onloop Posted at: 2015-12-22T08:43:50.781Z Reads: 204

```
After weeks of trial & error trying to develop the best wireless remote controller to pair with the Raptor a winner has been selected. I tried as many remotes as possible in order to get the best one currently available.

**The Goals.**
**1. Compact**
**2. Reliable**
**3. Rechargeable**
**4. Cruise Control**

In the end It came down to two remotes. The Wii Style with Custom PCB was a front runner, Lots of time & money was spent trying to get the Wii style remote perfected for consumer use! However we had to give up on that due to signal problems & drop outs. It just isn't safe enough yet, more work needs to be done on that.

**Here is the winner.**

2.4ghz, Compact, Rechargeable, Cruise Control - UBER RELIABLE!

This NEW Enertion remote feels nice in the hand due to the ergonomic shape & it also has a nice silicone grip for the hot sweaty days. Controlling Speed using this feels very safe, it has custom software that has ramp-up-control that makes it hard to accidentally accelerate too-rapidly, this feature makes the ride feel much more in-control & predictable. I think Raptor owners will appreciate how good this makes the Raptor Feel. It also has Cruise Control which works really well.
```
