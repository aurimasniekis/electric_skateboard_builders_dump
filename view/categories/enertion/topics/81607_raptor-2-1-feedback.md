```
We've got plenty of happy customers too, so remember not to take one opinion as the final. I've got two raptor twos of my own and they work great, though I'm coming from a very biased position working with Enertion so :man_shrugging:, I'm not really the type to lie about it though. The main issue we've been having is consistency, most boards work great and a minority have some issues. @barajabali and our team works hard everyday to make sure the issues that make it through our checks get fixed. We do still have a long way to go but none of us are giving up any time soon. 

The motors get hot, and are designed to run hot if you ride them hard. Pretty much all hub motors that output performance comparable to the raptor are going to get warm under intense riding.
```
