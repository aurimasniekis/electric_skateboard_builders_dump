## \#53 Posted by: Clonkex Posted at: 2017-09-13T23:25:03.559Z Reads: 68

```
Only issue I see with that setup is you will go over the 60k eRPM limit of the VESC. At 12S you'll be at 50.4v on full charge, which puts the theoretical RPM of the motor at 9,576. Assuming a 14-pole motor that's 9,576 * 7 pole pairs equals 67,032 eRPM. Not 100% certain that limit still applies (may have been fixed in the firmware, not sure) but people still talk about it as if it does so I'd be careful if I were you because if it isn't fixed your DRV chip will blow.
```
