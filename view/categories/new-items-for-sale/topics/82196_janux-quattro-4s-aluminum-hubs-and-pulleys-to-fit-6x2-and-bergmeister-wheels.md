# Janux “Quattro 4S” aluminum hubs and pulleys to fit 6x2 and Bergmeister wheels

Hubs are CNC machined from 3.5” diameter slug of USA 6061 T6511 aluminum alloy -  San Diego, CA USA.  

Designed to fit **6”x2” pneumatic street tires and Bergmeister wheels** with standard size 608 bearings, 2-piece hub with integrated 19mm or 0.75” M6 thread length, single hex tool is used for wheel assembly – no fumbling with small lock nuts or tools.  

Hubs come with **2 36t x 16mm POM pulleys** to be used with dual 15mm belts.  POM pulleys are CNC machined from solid rounds of Acetal Delrin not 3d printed.  Wide ranges of pulley sizes are also available from 32 to 70t, speedy to torquey setup.

Hubs can be fitted to regular Caliber II trucks running dual 6355 motors, TB218mm with dual 6374s, or any trucks with 8mm axles.

**Price for a set of 4 hubs is $275 includes:**
-	Set of 4 6061 T6511 aluminum hubs
-	Set of 2 36t x 16mm Acetal Delrin pulleys
-	4 hub spacers
-	Heavy duty Stainless steel M6 hex bolts for hubs assembly
-	Stainless steel 10-24 bolts for POM pulleys
-	**No bearings, customers supply their own** 
-	**Free shipping within the US**
-	Also available in anodized black at additional $50 per set

Available pulley options, set of 2:
