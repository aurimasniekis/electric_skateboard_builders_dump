```
Yes. I feel like Entortion is the IKEA of DIY electric skateboards. Because of them, there's a lot more people buying kits, assembling them, and calling them "DIY".

There's nothing wrong with that though. After all, people who assemble their own e-boards are just as happy as the people who build them from scratch.

The problem lies within this forum. Everyone who wants to build an electric skateboard ends up here, and since this forum is run by Entortion (correct me if I am wrong here), they are instantly sucked into the idea that they need to spend hundreds of dollars on Entortion products, like their motor mounts, motors, and their "platinum VESC's" in order to make a good e-board.

**No**. Go out, cut a piece of aluminum with a jigsaw, and weld it onto a pair of Paris trucks. There's your $15 motor mount. You've not only saved money, but you have also learned how to work with metal. Same thing goes for batteries. Go on HobbyKing, buy some LiPo's, and solder them together to a BMS. Congrats! You've made a SPACE cell, saved money, and learned valuable skills. 

Motor not suiting you? Do some research, go on different hobby sites, and find the cheapest motor that fits your specs. And what if you want the ultimate experience IMHO? **Solder your own VESC** :O You'll get a sweet ride, and you'll learn how to handle SMDs!
```
